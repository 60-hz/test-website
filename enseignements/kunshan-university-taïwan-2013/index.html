Kunshan University - Taïwan 2013

Interactive multimedia design class - Department of visual Communication Design

 

Six months multimedia class as invited teacher with the students of Visual Communication Design department, Kunshan University, 2013-2014. The final work was exhibited during a week in the university at the end of the year.

 

Organisation:

- 133 Students for a total of 20 teams (around 5/6 students per team)

- 21 projects

- Weekly Class of interactive media design

- One meeting every week in office with the Team members in order to follow the Team's projects

TOOLKIT for students

MEANDRE  (toolbox for real time multimedia design, based on Pure Data)
