Projection mapping technic

Projection-mapping techniques enable you to create visual art on physical surfaces using just a video projector. By connecting a single projector to a laptop and utilizing specialized software, you can project videos, images, and colors onto various surfaces. The software allows for real-time correction of projection distortions through deformable quadrilaterals, which can be adjusted directly on the screen by dragging their corners.
